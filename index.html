The Most Exciting Band Kerry Has Seen in Years

Chapter 2 are a high energy, highly entertaining, 4pc band based in Tralee in the Kingdom of Kerry.
They have developed a unique interpretive style and character to covers of classic rock tunes that everyone will know and appreciate.
They play a wide variety of songs from the 1960's up to the modern day with a strong preference for great rock anthems from the 70', 80's and 90's, all done with their unique twist and sound.
The band have been playing around the pub circuit in Kerry for a number of years now and have built a large and loyal fan base who follow them around to gigs all over the county. They have build an excellent reputation for providing a lively and musically accomplished performance whereever they play. Click the link below to see where you can see them playing soon!

In recent years the band have been regular invitees to The Troubadour Club, part of the Celebration of Christy Hennessy & Song-writers weekend and have participated in the Battle of the Bands run by The BarN @ Paud O'Neills in Killorglin in conjunction with K-Fest, winning the competition in 2019.
